Tabouleh

The healthiest and most delicious salad tabouleh

Everyone is busy now and don't have enough time for healthy food.But once you prepare this healthy salad you will catch yourself doing it again and again Tabouleh is a delight for your tongue and stomach

Ingredients

    bulgur wheat(soak beforehand) fresh tomatoes in vine lettuce bunch of parsley bunch of cilantro 4-5 spring onions 2-3 tablespoons of olive oil salt pepper lemon juice

Steps

    Choose medium or small bulgur wheat, pour it into the medium bowl . If it is small size, you can just add water . If medium,boil a minute and leave it to cool off. Prepare onions, parsley, cilantro ,lettuce. Rinse with fresh water. Take a large bowl. Chop the fresh greens. Send chopped to the large bowl. Add bulgur wheat. It's time for fresh juicy tasty tomatoes. Cut them into small pieces. Pour it into the bowl. Add salt, pepper, olive oil , lemon juice. Mix. Enjoy.
