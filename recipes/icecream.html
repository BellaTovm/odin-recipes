Easy Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 4 cups half-and-half or light cream
  • 1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRANDÂ® Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine half-and-half, condensed milk, and vanilla in a large bowl; mix well.
